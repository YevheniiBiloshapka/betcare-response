What is Cookie?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our Website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

What type of Cookie we use?

Necessary:
PHPSESSID: This cookie is essential for the proper functioning of the Website. It maintains the user's session, allowing the Website to recognize and remember you as you navigate through different pages. Without this cookie, certain features of the Website may not function correctly.

Analytics:
sbjs_migrations, sbjs_current_add, sbjs_first_add, sbjs_current, sbjs_first, sbjs_udata, sbjs_session: These cookies are essential for integrating third-party services or libraries related to user behaviour tracking and personalized content delivery. They contribute to enhancing user experience by providing relevant and targeted content based on individual preferences and interactions.

wp_mkg_uid: This cookie is used to track how a user interacts with marketing campaigns and to measure campaign effectiveness.

_gid, ga: These cookies are used by Google Analytics to collect statistics about user interactions on the Website.

Kindly be aware that the list mentioned above might not encompass all cookies in use on our Website, and additional cookies with similar purposes may be employed. Nevertheless, our commitment remains steadfast in offering transparency and safeguarding your privacy rights
